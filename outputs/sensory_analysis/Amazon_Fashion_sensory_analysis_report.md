## Initial Interpretation

- This subset helps narrow the full Amazon Fashion dataset toward products and reviews that are more relevant to sensory clothing concerns.
- The main performance indicators to compare are rating distribution, average rating, negative review percentage, and review volume.
- The complaint-term table gives a first-pass view of common issues, but later feature engineering should make this more systematic.
- Product-level insights should be treated carefully because many products in the full dataset have very few reviews.
